## 7. Prioritised List of Improvements

### Fix 1 — CRITICAL: Add the primary keyword and named secondary keywords more deliberately (density / placement)

The exact phrase "housewarming gifts for expats" appears only 4 times (0.28%). The minimum target is 15 occurrences. Add the exact phrase in at least 4–6 more natural locations, replacing loose variants where possible. Specific opportunities:

- Para 3 ("Finding the right housewarming gift for expats") — already close; tighten to exact phrase.
- "The best housewarming gifts for expats understand this." (Para 5) — already exact; keep.
- Section intro of "Sentimental gifts that go beyond the practical" — open with "Not every expat housewarming gift idea requires..." (adds secondary keyword too).
- "Practical gifts worth giving" section — add "For expats settling into a new home..." in first sentence.
- Add "gifts for expats new home" and "personalised gifts for expats" at least once each — they have zero occurrences despite being in the target list.

### Fix 2 — HIGH: Correct meta title length and rewrite meta description

The meta title at 65 characters will be truncated in SERPs. Shorten to under 60 characters while retaining the primary keyword. The meta description must also include the primary keyword (or at minimum "expats") and reach 150 characters. Current version (147 chars, no expat mention) fails both requirements.

**Suggested meta title** (56 chars): `Housewarming gifts for expats: ideas that mean something`

**Suggested meta description** (153 chars): `Housewarming gifts for expats deserve more than a candle. This guide covers the most thoughtful ones — including a personalised map print of the place they left behind.`

### Fix 3 — HIGH: Add keyword variation to at least one more H2 heading

Only 1 of 6 H2s contains a keyword variant. Two H2s are strong candidates for revision without disrupting the article's editorial tone:

- "How to choose: a simple framework" → "How to choose the right housewarming gift for an expat" (adds both target words; reads naturally as a section heading)
- Alternatively: "Taking a place with you" → "Taking a place with you: gifts for expats settling in" (adds secondary keyword angle)

Either change satisfies the 2+ H2 guideline.

### Fix 4 — MEDIUM: Add external links (currently 0; minimum required is 2)

The InterNations Expat Insider survey is referenced in the body but not hyperlinked. This is the most authoritative claim in the article and should be cited properly. Add a live hyperlink to the relevant InterNations or Expat Insider page. A second external link to a credible source on the personalised gifts market (SkyQuest, Grand View Research, or similar) would satisfy the 2-link minimum and strengthen E-E-A-T trustworthiness signals.

### Fix 5 — MEDIUM: Remove duplicate internal links and enrich anchor text for style pages

Two destination URLs are linked twice each (`/gifts/housewarming` and `/create`), violating the guidelines' one-link-per-page rule. Remove or replace one instance of each. The freed link slot should be used to link to `/styles` with an anchor like "explore all eight map styles" — this adds destination variety and serves readers who want to browse beyond the three highlighted styles. Also consider enriching "Paper style", "Mineral style", and "Modern style" anchors to "the Paper style map print" etc., which are more descriptive and keyword-adjacent.
